### Problem 1

Back in Ye Olden Tymes, 5.25" floppy disks produced by Disks R Us had a 1% chance of being defective, with each disk’s risk independent of the others. Disks were sold in packs of 10. Take a time machine back…

  1. The company offers a money-back guarantee that at most 1 disk in a pack will be defective. Any pack that exceeds this amount can be returned for a full refund. What is the chance that you buy only one pack and qualify for a refund?
prob <- pbinom(8,10,.99)
print(prob)
## [1] 0.0042662
  1. Suppose you bought 10 packs of disks. Each pack is independent of the others. What is the chance that you could claim a refund on more than one pack?
pbinom(2, 10, prob, lower.tail = FALSE)
## [1] 9.111051e-06
